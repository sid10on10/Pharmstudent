Instructions to authors                                                       Download instructions to authors (pdf)                                                                       

The Pharmstudent is an official scientific publication of Pharmaceutical Society, Department of Pharmaceutics, IIT (BHU) and it was the first such journal started in India in the year 1934. This journal publishes research and review articles covering the broad areas of pharmaceutical sciences. The Pharmstudent is published annually in both print and online versions.

Submission of a manuscript to Pharmstudent for publication implies that the same has not been either published or under consideration in another journal. The author should confirm this during submission of manuscript. All manuscripts should be submitted via e-mail to the editor's e mail i.d.: pharmstudent@itbhu.ac.in

Manuscripts should be prepared in A4 size paper with 1 inch margin on all the sides using MS Word processor (Office 2003 and above). The manuscript must contain the following contents in the order listed below.

Title Page
It should contain title, author's names and affiliations and corresponding author's email ID. The title must be in bold, upper case and 14 font. Authors’ names in bold lower case 12 fonts and their affiliation in lower case (not bold).

Body of manuscript
Review article should contain abstract (not more than 200 words), keywords (not more than 5), text of article, conclusion and references. Research article should contain abstract (not more than 200 words), keywords (not more than 5), introduction, materials and methods, results and discussion, conclusion, future prospective, acknowledgements and references. The heading must be in 12 font bold upper case. The subheading must be in 12 font bold lower case. Further subheading should be in 12 font bold lower case italics. The remaining text must be in Times Roman 12 font with 1.5 line spacing.

Tables and figures
The tables and figures (jpg, tiff, png format with resolution at least 96dpi), if present, should be numbered in Arabic numerals and must appear in the text in sequence. Font size of table content should be 10 and of figure and table captions should be 10, bold. All the figures should be embedded in text at appropriate places and should appear in sequence throughout the manuscript.

References
References should be numbered (Font size 10) in following manner.
 For journals:

Draize JH, Woodard J, Calvary HO. Methods for the study of irritation and toxicity of substances applied topically to the skin and mucous membranes. J Pharmacol Exp Therap.1944; 82: 377-89.
For books:

Gottman J. Time Series Analysis. Cambridge: CUP, 1981

For web sources:

http://www.patient.co.uk/medicine/Miconazole-oral.htm (accessed on 10 Nov 2009)
For Patents:

Basf AG. Means and methods for preventing and treating caries. WO2006027265 (2006)
Reference number should be given in text in parathesis before full stop or comma e.g.  ‘In addition to aerosol, transfersomes are also under consideration for delivery of insulin which is similar to that of liposomes with additional flexibility of encapsulating layer (42).’ Use et al., in case the authors’ names exceeds 2.

Note
The Editor in Chief does not claim any responsibility, liability for statements made and opinion expressed by author.
