About the Wall Magazine

The Pharmfanzine is an official quarterly Wall Magazine published by the Literary Wing of Pharmaceutical Society. It was started in the year 2008 and each issue includes the articles contributed by alumni, teachers and students of the department, details of papers published, conferences attended and other departmental and campus activities.
